5. Excis Compliance Performance Analysis

Excis Compliance France Monthly Performance

November 2025 Performance

Excis Compliance France generated $101,342 in revenue with a gross profit of $19,110, resulting in a 19% gross profit margin. This represents a slight decrease from October's 21% margin, primarily due to the ATOS_EC project where an engineer was on leave without backfill arrangements.

The reported loss of $3.5k in France is driven by the high cost of the engineer, Mahmoud Alhijjiri, combined with reduced income due to the absence of backfill during his leave period from 6 to 14 November. As noted by Adriana, this situation is currently under discussion with Dawn.

Excis Compliance France YTD Performance

Excis Compliance France has shown variable performance throughout 2025, with margins fluctuating between 19% and 21% in recent months. The entity has faced challenges with backfill arrangements and cost management, particularly in the ATOS_EC project.

For the YTD period, Excis Compliance France has contributed approximately $1.1M in revenue, representing about 3% of the company's total revenue. While this is a relatively small portion, the consistent margin challenges suggest a need for operational improvements.

Key Issues Identified

  • Backfill arrangements during employee leave periods are not properly managed, leading to margin erosion
  • High-cost engineers without corresponding revenue adjustments during leave periods
  • Lack of proactive measures to mitigate margin impact during known leave periods

9. Strategic Recommendations for 2026

1. Budget and Forecasting Process Improvement

Given the consistent 16% variance between budget and actual, we recommend a comprehensive review of our budgeting process:

  • Implement a more realistic budgeting approach based on historical performance and market conditions
  • Increase the frequency of budget reviews to quarterly rather than annually
  • Develop contingency plans for known cost fluctuations and market volatility
  • Enhance forecasting accuracy by incorporating project-specific risk factors

2. Margin Enhancement Strategy

To address the gap between our operating margin (6.2%) and industry benchmark (12%), we recommend:

  • Implement a tiered pricing model based on project complexity and risk
  • Develop standardized backfill arrangements with predefined costs and billing rates
  • Create a project profitability threshold of 20% gross margin for new projects
  • Establish a margin improvement target of 2 percentage points for 2026

3. Operational Excellence for Excis Compliance

Specifically for Excis Compliance France, we recommend:

  • Implement a proactive leave management system with automatic backfill arrangements
  • Develop standardized cost allocation methods for shared resources
  • Create a margin monitoring dashboard with alerts for underperforming projects
  • Establish a minimum gross profit margin threshold of 22% for all Excis Compliance projects

4. Portfolio Optimization

To address underperforming projects and countries, we recommend:

  • Conduct a comprehensive portfolio review with clear exit criteria for projects below 15% margin
  • Develop a strategic plan for underperforming countries (Angola, Bosnia) with specific turnaround targets
  • Implement a resource allocation model that prioritizes high-margin opportunities
  • Establish a quarterly portfolio review process with clear accountability

5. Cost Structure Optimization

To improve our cost efficiency, we recommend:

  • Conduct a comprehensive review of our cost structure with a target of 5% reduction in non-essential expenses
  • Implement a zero-based budgeting approach for operating expenses
  • Develop shared service models for common functions across countries
  • Establish a cost efficiency incentive program for project managers

6. Technology and Process Enhancement

To improve our operational efficiency, we recommend:

  • Implement an integrated project management system with real-time margin tracking
  • Develop automated alerts for margin erosion and cost overruns
  • Create a centralized resource management system for better allocation
  • Implement predictive analytics for project profitability
